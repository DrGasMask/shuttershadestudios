Sqish's Home

Sqish's Home is like the gm_construct of Classic Sandbox. It's 100% accurate when it comes to the place of where Sqish lives and it contains a lot. Very great map to play with your friends on when playing for the first time!

Easter Eggs

Easter eggs are a very fun part of maps, from hidden lore to random scenery. This map has the most. There are books in-game that have fully made front and back covers, and of course, a spine. Look around for things and have a laugh!
